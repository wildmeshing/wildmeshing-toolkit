◆ collapse_edge()

auto wmtk::TriMesh::collapse_edge ( const Tuple &  t,
Accessor< long > &  hash_accessor 
)

collapse edge t

The vertex in t is collapsed towards the one where the tuple is pointing to. The returned tuple contains the remaining vertex. The edge represents the face of t that was collapsed. The face is chosen such that the orientation of the tuple is the same as in the input. If this is not possible due to a boundary, the opposite face is chosen.
