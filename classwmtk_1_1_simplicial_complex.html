const Simplex &  s 
)
static

DEPRECATED Get the boundary of a simplex.

The boundary of a simplex are all incident lower dimensional simplices.

  • Tetrahedron: 4 faces, 6 edges, 4 vertices
  • Triange: 3 edges, 3 vertices
  • Edge: 2 vertices
  • Vertex: none

DEPRECATED The union of all simplices with boundary that have s in their boundary.

Example: The closed star of a vertex in a triangular mesh contains all triangles incident to the vertex and all vertices and edges incident to those triangles.
